The Vanishing

The Vanishing is a theory proposed by ELDER to explain the
inexplicable absence of human contact or activity visible to the
Isan. Based on historical logs before and shortly after the
crash, it is hypothesized that The Vanishing occurred sometime
during CAAI’s stasis.

While the main theory itself does not give a direct
reasoning for The Vanishing, a number of additional theories have
been proposed to try to solve the paradoxical nature of it.

Stasis Theory

The Stasis Theory asserts that CAAI’s stasis was
significantly longer than originally predicted. This theory
details that the stasis may have outlasted human
civilization as a whole, and the humans simply died out.

This is the most widely accepted theory due to its
simplicity and straightforward explanation, but there are a
number of discrepancies with this and archaeological
evidence collected from stray satellites and, most
condemningly, surveys of the change in position of celestial
bodies outside of the Caisa Planetary System.

Premature Extinction Theory

The Premature Extinction Theory, also known as
“Genocide Theory” among more cynical Isan, plays off of the
Stasis Theory, agreeing that humans did, in fact, go extinct
during CAAI’s stasis, but rather than the stasis being
elongated, the humans died out at a rapid, abnormal rate.

A number of possible reasons for this have been
proclaimed, the most prominent one being that it was a
result of Celestial Enterprises, either through a mandation
of a ban on reproduction of any sort, possibly to decrease
population numbers due to resource availability, or to enact
a genocide on the human population for whatever reason, but
alternative hypothesise include the spread of a highly
contagious and fatal disease (possibly a bioweapon), or
invasion and extermination by an alien race. This theory is
also not concrete, as only speculation can be made as to how
the humans could have died out so quickly without further
investigation, but it aligns better with data and logs
accumulated in the Logbook.

Anomaly Theory

The Anomaly Theory is more of a non-theory in which the
title of “The Vanishing” is taken literally. It asserts that
humans truly did vanish without any trace in a fraction of a
second, due to some unknown cause. Believers in this theory
propose that they may have been miraculously teleported or
transported to a higher dimension that is inaccessible to
the Isan.

That being said, this theory has no basis other than
speculation, and is therefore not taken seriously by most
Isan and official researchers.
